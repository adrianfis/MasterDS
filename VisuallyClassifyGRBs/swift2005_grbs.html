Old Swift Trigger and Burst Information: 2005

This table contains information about GRBs (and non-GRBs) detected by Swift (BAT, XRT, and UVOT). The most recent Notice is listed first in the table (reverse time order).

The 3 left-most columns (GRB/Trigger) are the Trigger Number, the Date (yy/mm/dd) and the Time (hh:mm:ss.ss) of the start of the GRB.

The next 20 columns (Observation) come from the results of the various observations and analyses by the Swift BAT, XRT and UVOT instruments. These columns contain: the BAT RA,Dec Position and error, XRT RA,Dec Position and error, UVOT RA,Dec Position and error, the BAT LightCurve (links to the 4 std formats), XRT LightCurve (links), XRT Spectrum (types 1 and 2, links, both raw files and the post-processed), XRT Image (links, both raw files and the post-processed), UVOT Source_List (links, both raw files and the post-processed), UVOT Image (links, both raw files and the post-processed). All 3 RA,Dec locations are in J2000 epoch. The error (arcmin or arcsec diameter, systematic+statistical) uncertainty in that location. If a column's information is not available or is not applicable, then that entry is listed as "n/a" or left blank.
