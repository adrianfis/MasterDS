      the BAT RA,Dec Position and error,   XRT RA,Dec Position and error,   UVOT RA,Dec Position and error,

All 3 RA,Dec locations are in J2000 epoch. The error (arcmin or arcsec diameter, systematic+statistical) uncertainty in that location.
If a column's information is not available or is not applicable, then that entry is listed as "n/a" or left blank.
