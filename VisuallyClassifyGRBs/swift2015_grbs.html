The most recent Notice is listed first in the table (reverse time order).

      the Trigger Number (which is a link to all the original full-format email GCN/Swift Notices on that trigger),

      the Time (hh:mm:ss.ss) of the start of the GRB.

The next 20 columns (OBSERVATION) come from the results of the various observations and analyses by the Swift BAT, XRT and UVOT instruments.

      the BAT RA,Dec Position and error,   XRT RA,Dec Position and error,   UVOT RA,Dec Position and error,

      UVOT Image (links, both raw files and the post-processed).
All 3 RA,Dec locations are in J2000 epoch. The error (arcmin or arcsec diameter, systematic+statistical) uncertainty in that location.
If a column's information is not available or is not applicable, then that entry is listed as "n/a" or left blank.
